# IFAC Journal submission checklist

Source: IFAC Journal of Systems and Control Guide for Authors, checked on
2026-06-26.

- [ ] Confirm this is submitted through the SYSID journal-option workflow, or
      confirm that the SYSID submission does not count as simultaneous
      consideration elsewhere.
- [ ] Designate Diego Eckhard as corresponding author and provide full contact
      details in the submission system.
- [ ] Upload manuscript source and generated PDF.
- [ ] Upload separate figure files from `experiments/`.
- [ ] Upload `highlights.txt`.
- [ ] Complete the Elsevier declaration of competing interests form.
- [ ] Enter funding statement.
- [ ] Enter data/software availability statement.
- [ ] Enter CRediT author contribution statement.
- [ ] Disclose the arXiv preprint: `https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26376`.
- [ ] Decide whether to request/decline Elsevier SSRN preprint posting, given
      that the manuscript is already on arXiv.
- [ ] Confirm all references cited in the manuscript appear in the bibliography
      and vice versa.
- [ ] Run `make` in this directory before submission.
